Expert Tips

Insider CELPIP Strategies From Our Coaches

The exact techniques our top-scoring students use on exam day.

01
⏱️

Manage Your Time by Task

CELPIP is strictly timed. Practise switching tasks at exactly the right moment — even if you haven't finished. Unanswered questions score zero; attempted ones might score 1–5.

02
📋

Study the Scoring Descriptors

Download the official scoring descriptors and learn what separates a 9 from a 10. Examiners follow these exactly — so should you in every practice session.

03
🎙️

Speak for the Full Time — Always

Silence is penalised. If you finish speaking early, expand with examples, alternatives, or caveats. A fluent monologue that fills the time always scores higher.

04
✉️

Email Task: Address All 3 Points

The email task gives 3 bullet points. Students who address all 3 thoroughly, in distinct paragraphs, consistently score 2–3 bands higher than those who skip one.

05
📝

Listening: Note Keywords, Not Sentences

Write keywords and numbers only — never full sentences. Your brain processes better when you listen actively and note only essentials.

06
📚

Reading: Answers Follow Passage Order

CELPIP reading answers almost always follow the order of the passage. If stuck on Q14, the answer is between where Q13 and Q15 were found.
